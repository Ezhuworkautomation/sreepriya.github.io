My Approach

Where policy meets ground-level impact

I work at the intersection of policy, partnerships, and on-the-ground implementation — helping organisations navigate India's sustainability landscape with clarity, credibility, and measurable outcomes.

Whether you're structuring a carbon credit pathway for smallholder farmers, writing a grant for a bilateral funder, or trying to figure out how to tell your climate story to investors — I've been there, done it, and can help you do it faster. Think of me as your sustainability co-pilot.

I've worked across the full stakeholder spectrum — bilateral agencies like GIZ and UNDP, multinationals like IKEA, on-ground organisations like Karo Sambhav and Krushi Sarathi, and remote communities in Ladakh. I've also hosted a top-20 global circular economy podcast reaching listeners in 50 countries.
